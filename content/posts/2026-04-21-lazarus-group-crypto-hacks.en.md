### Operational Methodology: Disciplined, Patient, and Structured

North Korea no longer simply attacks from the outside. They penetrate the heart of the ecosystem through the following tactics:

**1. The Long Game**
The *TraderTraitor* unit applies for roles at major DeFi projects using fake identities (proxies) from China or Southeast Asia. They work diligently, contribute code, and build trust over months to gain access to multisig keys or governance. Once the target is ripe, they execute from within.

**2. China as a Primary Proxy**
Many North Korean IT workers reside in Chinese border cities like Dandong. They utilize Chinese internet infrastructure and local identities to open bank accounts or exchange accounts. Stolen funds are laundered through mixers and OTC brokers in China to be converted into fiat currency.

**3. Supply Chain Manipulation**
The Bybit case ($1.5B) proves their expertise in supply chain manipulation. They impersonate recruiters on LinkedIn, deliver malware-laden technical tests to target developers, or pose as strategic investors to infiltrate systems.

**4. Focus on DeFi and Bridges**
DeFi protocols and cross-chain bridges are primary targets due to massive liquidity concentration without centralized oversight. Robbing these protocols is far more efficient than penetrating traditional banking systems monitored by SWIFT.

Crypto security is no longer just about code exploits; it is about the integrity of the human resources behind the code.
